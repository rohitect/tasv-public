TASV ("The Advanced Smart Vault") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

üì± Information We Access

Device Media Access

TASV is a gallery and vault application that requires access to your device's photos, videos, and media files to function. We access:

  • Photos and Images: To display and manage your photo library
  • Videos: To display and play your video files
  • Media Location Data: To read EXIF GPS data embedded in your photos for location-based organization and map view features

Local Storage

We store the following data locally on your device:

  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences within the app
  • Favorites: List of media items you've marked as favorites
  • Ratings: Any ratings you assign to your media
  • Custom Albums: Albums you create to organize your media
  • Vault Data: Encrypted vault contents, vault PINs (hashed), folder structures, and access logs
  • Biometric Settings: Your preference for biometric unlock (fingerprint/face)

Vault Feature

TASV includes a secure vault feature that allows you to:

  • Create multiple password-protected vaults
  • Organize vault contents into folders
  • Move photos and videos to secure storage
  • Track access logs for security monitoring

All vault data is stored locally on your device with PIN protection. Vault PINs are stored as hashed values and cannot be recovered if forgotten.

üö´ Information We Do NOT Collect

TASV is designed with privacy as a priority:

  • No Data Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers
  • No Analytics: We do not use third-party analytics services
  • No Advertising: We do not display advertisements or share data with advertisers
  • No Account Required: We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information
  • No Cloud Sync: Your media, vault contents, and preferences remain on your device only
  • No Backup to Cloud: Vault contents are never uploaded to any cloud service

üìã Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions:

Permission Purpose
READ_MEDIA_IMAGES Access photos in your gallery
READ_MEDIA_VIDEO Access videos in your gallery

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION Show your current location on the map view

üîí Data Security

All your data remains on your device:

  • Media files are accessed directly from your device storage
  • Vault contents are stored in the app's private directory
  • Vault PINs are hashed before storage (cannot be reversed)
  • Preferences are stored locally using secure storage mechanisms
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • Biometric authentication uses your device's secure hardware

üîê Vault Security

  • Access logging for security auditing
  • Automatic log retention management
  • Secure file storage in app-private directories
