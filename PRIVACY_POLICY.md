# Privacy Policy for TASV

**Last Updated:** December 22, 2024

## Introduction

TASV ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles your information.

## Information We Access

### Device Media Access
TASV is a gallery application that requires access to your device's photos, videos, and media files to function. We access:

- **Photos and Images:** To display and manage your photo library
- **Videos:** To display and play your video files
- **Media Location Data:** To read EXIF GPS data embedded in your photos for location-based organization (optional feature)

### Local Storage
We store the following data locally on your device:
- **App Preferences:** Your settings and preferences within the app
- **Favorites:** List of media items you've marked as favorites
- **Ratings:** Any ratings you assign to your media

## Information We Do NOT Collect

TASV is designed with privacy as a priority:

- **No Data Collection:** We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers
- **No Analytics:** We do not use third-party analytics services
- **No Advertising:** We do not display advertisements or share data with advertisers
- **No Account Required:** We do not require you to create an account or provide personal information
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your media and preferences remain on your device only

## Permissions Used

The app requires the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|------------|---------|
| READ_MEDIA_IMAGES | Access photos in your gallery |
| READ_MEDIA_VIDEO | Access videos in your gallery |
| READ_MEDIA_AUDIO | Access audio files (for future features) |
| READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE | Access media on older Android versions (API < 33) |
| WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE | Save edited images (API < 30) |
| ACCESS_MEDIA_LOCATION | Read GPS location from photo EXIF data |
| SET_WALLPAPER | Set images as device wallpaper |
| INTERNET | Reserved for potential future features |

## Data Security

All your data remains on your device:
- Media files are accessed directly from your device storage
- Preferences are stored locally using secure storage mechanisms
- No data is transmitted over the internet

## Third-Party Services

TASV does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

TASV does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app simply provides access to media already stored on the device.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete from our systems. All your data remains under your control on your device.
